The PlayStation 2 (abbreviated PS2) is a stationary game console released by Sony Computer Entertainment on March 4, 2000 in Japan. The PlayStation 2 is the second game console from the PlayStation brand. Production ceased in Japan on December 29, 2012 after more than 12 years. The PlayStation 2 was in direct competition with the Nintendo GameCube, the Sega Dreamcast, and Microsoft's Xbox.
